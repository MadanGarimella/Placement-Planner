General Preparation Strategy (Ongoing) 🗓️

These are crucial strategies to follow throughout your 3-month journey to maximize your chances of success.

Overall Skill Allocation

A visual representation of how your efforts might be distributed across key learning areas over the entire 3-month period.

1. Build a Strong Online Presence

  • Active GitHub with well-documented projects.
  • Professional and updated LinkedIn profile.
  • Portfolio Website (Optional but Recommended).

2. Project Portfolio is Key

  • 1-2 solid full-stack projects.
  • Explain technical choices, challenges, and solutions.
  • Be ready to demo and walk through code.

3. DSA, DSA, DSA

  • Crucial for MNC screening and interviews.
  • Practice consistently: solve problems daily.
  • Understand concepts and trade-offs.

4. Master Core Concepts

  • How the web works (HTTP, DNS, APIs).
  • Basic software engineering principles (DRY, KISS).

5. Behavioral Interviews

  • Prepare for common questions (STAR method).
  • Research company culture and values.

6. Networking

  • Connect with industry professionals.
  • Engage in online developer communities.

7. Stay Updated

  • Follow tech blogs, news, and influencers.
  • Be aware of emerging trends.

8. Practice Aptitude Tests

  • Quantitative, Logical Reasoning, Verbal Ability.

9. Mock Interviews - The Secret Weapon

  • Practice extensively with peers or platforms.
  • Focus on articulating your thought process.

Important Considerations

  • Consistency: Stick to your schedule. Focused daily work is key.
  • Hands-on Practice: Prioritize building and solving over passive learning.
  • Don't Get Stuck: Ask for help when needed. Move on and revisit.
  • Rest & Revision: Take breaks and regularly revise learned concepts.
  • Adaptability: Adjust this template to your learning pace and prior knowledge.

This journey requires dedication and perseverance. Stay focused, celebrate small wins, and believe in your ability to achieve your goal! Good luck!
